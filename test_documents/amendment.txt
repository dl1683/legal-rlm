FIRST AMENDMENT TO SERVICE AGREEMENT

This First Amendment ("Amendment") to the Service Agreement dated January 15, 2024 
("Original Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2024 by and between:

ACME Corporation ("Client")
and
TechServices LLC ("Provider")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the parties entered into the Original Agreement for software development services; and
WHEREAS, the parties desire to amend certain terms of the Original Agreement.

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree to amend the Original Agreement as follows:

1. AMENDMENTS TO COMPENSATION

Section 3.1 of the Original Agreement is hereby deleted in its entirety and replaced with 
the following:

"3.1 Fees. Client shall pay Provider a monthly fee of $65,000 for the Services (increased 
from $50,000), effective March 1, 2024. Payment shall be made within thirty (30) days of 
receipt of invoice."

Reason for increase: Additional scope of work including mobile application development 
and 24/7 support services.

2. AMENDMENTS TO SCOPE OF SERVICES

The following services are added to Exhibit A:
   - Mobile application development (iOS and Android)
   - 24/7 technical support and maintenance
   - Cloud infrastructure management
   - Security audits and penetration testing

3. AMENDMENTS TO TERM

Section 2.1 of the Original Agreement is hereby amended to extend the term by an additional 
six (6) months, for a total term of eighteen (18) months from the Effective Date of the 
Original Agreement.

4. ADDITIONAL OBLIGATIONS

4.1 Service Level Agreement. Provider agrees to maintain 99.9% uptime for all production 
systems and respond to critical issues within two (2) hours.

4.2 Dedicated Team. Provider shall assign a dedicated team of at least five (5) full-time 
developers to the project, including one (1) senior architect.

5. INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

Provider shall maintain the following insurance coverage:
   - Professional Liability Insurance: $2,000,000 per occurrence
   - Cyber Liability Insurance: $5,000,000 per occurrence
   - General Liability Insurance: $1,000,000 per occurrence

Provider shall provide certificates of insurance to Client within fifteen (15) days of 
the effective date of this Amendment.

6. PERFORMANCE METRICS

The parties agree to the following key performance indicators (KPIs):
   - Code quality: Minimum 80% test coverage
   - Delivery: 95% of milestones delivered on time
   - Bug resolution: Critical bugs resolved within 24 hours
   - Customer satisfaction: Minimum score of 4.5/5.0 in quarterly reviews

Failure to meet these KPIs for two consecutive quarters shall entitle Client to terminate 
this Agreement with thirty (30) days notice.

7. RATIFICATION

Except as expressly amended herein, all terms and conditions of the Original Agreement 
remain in full force and effect.

8. COUNTERPARTS

This Amendment may be executed in counterparts, each of which shall be deemed an original 
and all of which together shall constitute one and the same instrument.
